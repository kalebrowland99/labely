Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

Labely ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). Please read this policy carefully.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Name and email address when you create an account or sign in via Google.
  • Scan history: Product barcodes and QR codes you scan within the App.
  • Onboarding responses: Health preferences and goals you provide during setup (e.g., dietary concerns, symptoms).
  • Device information: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers for analytics and crash reporting.
  • Usage data: How you interact with features of the App (e.g., screens viewed, buttons tapped).
  • Purchase data: Subscription status managed through Apple's In-App Purchase system and RevenueCat.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize the product safety and ingredient scanning experience.
  • To maintain and improve the App.
  • To process subscriptions and manage your account.
  • To send important service-related communications (e.g., verification codes, billing notices).
  • To measure and analyze App performance and usage trends.
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which may have access to your information:

  • Firebase (Google) – Authentication, database storage, and analytics. Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat – Subscription management and in-app purchase tracking. Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In – Optional social sign-in. Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI – AI-powered ingredient and product analysis. Privacy Policy
  • Open Food Facts – Open-source product ingredient database. Privacy Policy
  • Meta (Facebook SDK) – Advertising attribution and conversion measurement. Privacy Policy
  • Mixpanel – Product analytics. Privacy Policy

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with third parties only in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers listed above who help operate the App.
  • If required by law or to protect our legal rights.
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (you will be notified).

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at support@labely.app.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of certain data processing activities.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@labely.app.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
